The Complex Hotel Enterprise Network Architecture and Design Project provides a scalable and secure infrastructure tailored for the high-demand environment of modern hospitality. The design integrates diverse operational departments—including IT, Management, Finance, and Guest Services—into a unified system that prioritizes high availability and seamless service delivery. By incorporating dual ISPs with automatic failover and redundant core layers, the architecture ensures continuous connectivity and minimizes potential downtime, meeting the essential business requirements for operational efficiency and a premium guest experience.

To maintain robust security and performance, the network utilizes logical segmentation through VLANs to isolate departmental traffic and a Next-Generation Firewall to enforce strict access control and intrusion prevention. The infrastructure leverages Layer 3 switches for efficient inter-VLAN routing and a centralized wireless controller to manage high-speed, seamless roaming across guest rooms and public areas. Supporting technologies such as Power over Ethernet (PoE) and VoIP are deployed throughout the facility, ensuring reliable internal communication and a simplified hardware footprint for access points and IP telephony.
